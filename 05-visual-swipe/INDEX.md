## Key Visual Patterns

### 1. AI Illustration (RejuvaCare dominant)
- Warm, painterly style (Midjourney aesthetic)
- Couples in domestic settings (bed, couch, kitchen)
- Pain visualized through body language, not graphics
- Soft color palette: warm yellows, muted blues

### 2. Clinical/Medical
- X-ray imagery, anatomical diagrams
- Doctor/lab coat authority figures
- Clean white backgrounds

### 3. UGC/Native (Ugly Ad)
- Low-production phone photos
- Real people, real environments
- No studio lighting or polish

### 4. Product-in-Use
- Device on body in real home setting
- Lifestyle context (couch, bed, kitchen)
- Emphasizes ease and normalcy

### 5. Shock/Pattern Interrupt
- Surgery close-ups
- Extreme before/after comparisons
- Designed to stop the scroll cold
