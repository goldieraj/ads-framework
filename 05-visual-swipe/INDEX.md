## Key Visual Patterns Identified

### 1. AI Illustration (RejuvaCare dominant)
- Warm, painterly style (Midjourney/DALL-E aesthetic)
- Couples in domestic settings (bed, couch, kitchen)
- Pain visualized through body language, not graphics
- Soft color palette: warm yellows, muted blues

### 2. Medical/Clinical (HeartCalm, Neuropathy)
- X-ray images showing joint damage
- Clinical device photography
- Before/after comparison layouts
- Medical professional imagery

### 3. "Ugly Ad" Native Style (BioRoot Labs)
- iPhone-quality selfie photos
- Dr Ruth White on beach, in garden
- Deliberately low-production to blend with organic posts
- Text-light, image-heavy

### 4. Product Hero + Text Overlay (Gadgets Club)
- Product on white/clean background
- Bold text overlay with benefit claim
- Price or discount badge
- German-language variants for EU targeting

### 5. Shock/Pattern Interrupt (Neuropathy niche)
- Graphic foot/hand images showing nerve damage
- Before/after of skin conditions
- Medical procedure imagery
- High scroll-stopping power

## Usage Notes

- Images are 600x600px (Meta Ad Library standard display size)
- All images are from inactive or currently running ads (as of June 2026)
- Use as reference for creative direction, NOT for direct reuse
- Pair with copy vault entries for full ad reconstruction
