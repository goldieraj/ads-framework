Most of what we do for shoulder pain doesn't work long-term.

There. I said it.

We stretch. We strengthen. We give you exercises to do at home.

Same shoulder. Same pain. Same disappointed look on their faces.

"I did everything you told me. Why does it still hurt?"

Diane. Retired teacher. 61 years old. The kindest woman you'd ever meet.

We did everything by the book. Stretches. Strengthening. Ultrasound. E-stim.

Eight months of copays. Eight months of driving to my clinic. Eight months of hope.

And her shoulder was maybe 15% better.

One day she looked at me and said:

"I just want to be able to hang my clothes on the line again. Is that too much to ask?"
