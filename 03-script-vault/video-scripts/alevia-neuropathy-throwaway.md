# Alevia Video Script: "The Throwaway"

**Score:** 9/10
**Duration:** 55 seconds (~0:55)
**Hook Type:** Extreme Visual Action + Specific Curiosity Gap | **Format:** UGC / Native

**Notes for AI Strategist:** Use this script template when selling a health or pain-relief product where the target audience has already tried (and failed with) the mainstream, expensive solutions. The core strategy is validating their failure and offering a contrarian fix.

---

## 1. The Hook (0:00 - 0:05)
**Goal:** Stop the scroll with a shocking visual action that contradicts normal behavior.

**Visual:** Woman literally throwing a large, expensive-looking $300 foot massager machine into a trash can.
**Voiceover:** "Why did I just throw away my $300 foot massager?"

## 2. The Contrarian Pivot (0:05 - 0:15)
**Goal:** Answer the hook question with a statement that challenges what the viewer believes about their problem.

**Visual:** Woman sitting on bed, holding up her bare feet, pointing to the soles.
**Voiceover:** "Because I finally found out why my feet were burning and tingling every night, and I learned that massaging them actually makes the damage worse."

## 3. The Mechanism Education (0:15 - 0:30)
**Goal:** Give the viewer a new vocabulary for their pain. Explain *why* the old methods failed.

**Visual:** Split screen. Left side: Woman holding Alevia cream. Right side: Simple 3D animation of nerve endings looking frayed and red.
**Voiceover:** "It's nerve damage, not muscle damage. You have to heal the myelin sheath—the protective coating around the nerve. Rubbing the skin or squeezing the muscle doesn't reach the nerves."

## 4. The Solution & Claim (0:30 - 0:45)
**Goal:** Introduce the product as the specific answer to the newly defined mechanism.

**Visual:** Close-up of the woman rubbing the cream onto her feet. Text overlay: "Penetrates 4 Layers Deep."
**Voiceover:** "This Alevia cream has a specific compound that penetrates 4 layers deep to actually cool the nerves and start repairing the sheath. It doesn't just numb the pain, it targets the root cause."

## 5. The Lifestyle Proof & Soft CTA (0:45 - 0:55)
**Goal:** Show the emotional payoff (sleep) and offer a low-friction next step.

**Visual:** Woman sleeping peacefully in bed. Text overlay: "First full night's sleep in 3 years."
**Voiceover:** "I haven't woken up with burning feet in three weeks. If you're dealing with the pins and needles, click below to see the ingredients and the clinical studies."
