# Hermes Video Ad Framework

## The 5-Beat Structure (Validated across 500+ Ads)

1. **The Hook (0:00-0:05)**
   - *Goal*: Stop the scroll, agitate the problem, create a curiosity gap.
   - *Best Practice*: The highest converting hooks combine Confession + Conspiracy (e.g., "I spent 23 years believing the opposite. They want this taken down.").
   - *Alternative*: The Math Hook. "Your doctor hears 60 of your 86,400 daily heartbeats." Irrefutable urgency.
   - *Avoid*: Product shots in the first 3 seconds. Problem-first always beats product-first.

2. **The Villain Pivot (0:05-0:15)**
   - *Goal*: Shift blame off the viewer and validate their pain.
   - *Best Practice*: Name a common enemy the audience already suspects (e.g., "The $400/session clinics that ignore you 364 days a year").

3. **The Mechanism Reveal (0:15-0:30)**
   - *Goal*: Explain *why* the product works using simple science. This is where skepticism is crushed.
   - *Archetype 1 (The Starvation Mechanism)*: "Your body isn't broken, it's starving. It needs blood flow/nutrients." Reframes the problem from repair (hard) to nourishment (easy).
   - *Archetype 2 (The Hidden Cause)*: "Your dog isn't slowing down because of age. It's because of [Specific Toxin]."
   - *Best Practice*: Use a 3D medical animation sync or visual diagram to make the mechanism intuitively true.

4. **The Proof Stacking (0:30-0:45)**
   - *Goal*: Build undeniable trust through visual demonstration.
   - *Physical Products*: The "Wind Test" — demonstrate the product's power through its effect on surrounding objects (e.g., curtains blowing), not just the user.
   - *Health/Beauty*: The "Live Wipe" (real-time application) or rapid-fire UGC testimonials.

5. **The Stacking CTA (0:45-End)**
   - *Goal*: Drive immediate action.
   - *Best Practice*: Offer + Urgency + Risk Reversal + Price Anchor.
   - *The Ultimate Price Anchor*: "$38,000 Surgery vs. $80 Device. Same Shoulder. Different Outcome." Find the most expensive alternative and anchor against it.

## Production Craft Rules

- **The "Constructed Authority Persona"**: Personas like "Dr. Blane Schilling, MD" are engineered, not found. Give them a specific backstory (years of experience, number of procedures) and a confession arc.
- **The "Frankenstein Ad"**: The dominant production method for elite ads. 1-2 original product shots + 80% stock footage + AI voiceover (ElevenLabs) + AI avatar (HeyGen) + text overlays. No traditional shoot required.
- **Specificity Devices**: Odd numbers are more believable. 87% is better than 90%. $863 is better than $900. "12 minutes before bed" creates a usage ritual and removes the "will I use it?" objection.
- **Scale Strategy**: Once a body copy/angle works, test creatives relentlessly. The best brands run 100+ creative variants of the same core message.
