# Retargeting Creative Strategy: The Warm Audience Protocol

This document outlines the strategy for creating ads specifically designed for users who have already interacted with the brand but haven't purchased (Retargeting/Bottom of Funnel).

## 1. The Psychology of Retargeting

Cold traffic ads must introduce the problem, mechanism, and solution. Retargeting ads assume the user already knows the brand and the product. The goal shifts from *education* to *objection handling* and *urgency creation*.

Why didn't they buy the first time?
1.  **Trust:** They don't believe it works (Needs Social Proof/Authority).
2.  **Price/Value:** They think it's too expensive (Needs Offer/Urgency).
3.  **Procrastination:** They meant to buy but got distracted (Needs a Reminder/Scarcity).
4.  **Specific Objection:** They have a specific concern (e.g., "Will it fit?", "Is it safe?") (Needs FAQ/Objection Handling).

## 2. The 4 Retargeting Creative Pillars

Hermes generates retargeting creative based on these four pillars.

### Pillar 1: The "Unboxing & Experience" (Overcoming Skepticism)
*   **Format:** UGC (User Generated Content) style, raw, unpolished.
*   **Focus:** Show exactly what arrives in the mail, how to open it, and the immediate first use.
*   **Copy Angle:** "I finally got my hands on [Product]..." or "Here's what actually happens when you order..."
*   **Why it works:** Demystifies the purchase and builds trust through transparency.

### Pillar 2: The "Rapid-Fire FAQ" (Objection Handling)
*   **Format:** Text-heavy, fast-paced video, or a clean carousel/static image.
*   **Focus:** Directly address the top 3 reasons people *don't* buy.
*   **Copy Angle:** "Still on the fence? Here are the top 3 questions we get..."
*   **Why it works:** Removes the final friction points preventing the sale.

### Pillar 3: The "Authority Endorsement" (Building Trust)
*   **Format:** "Press feature" style or "Expert review" style.
*   **Focus:** Leverage third-party credibility. Use quotes from doctors, publications, or massive view counts.
*   **Copy Angle:** "Why [Publication/Expert] called this the best [Category] of the year..."
*   **Why it works:** Borrows authority to validate the user's initial interest.

### Pillar 4: The "Irresistible Push" (Offer & Urgency)
*   **Format:** Static image or short, punchy graphic video.
*   **Focus:** The offer, the guarantee, and the scarcity.
*   **Copy Angle:** "We saved your cart. Take 15% off before it expires tonight." or "Risk-free for 90 days. What do you have to lose?"
*   **Why it works:** Forces a decision *now* rather than later.

## 3. Retargeting Ad Structure (Video)

Retargeting videos should be shorter than cold ads (typically 15-30 seconds).

1.  **The Recognition Hook (0:00-0:03):** Acknowledge they know you. (e.g., "Still thinking about [Product]?")
2.  **The Objection Smash (0:03-0:15):** Hit one of the 4 pillars (FAQ, Unboxing, Authority).
3.  **The De-risked CTA (0:15-0:20):** Emphasize the guarantee and the specific retargeting offer.

## 4. Retargeting Ad Structure (Image)

Static images are highly effective for retargeting.

*   **Visual:** High-quality product shot, often with a 5-star review overlay or a "Press" badge.
*   **Headline:** Direct and offer-focused (e.g., "Try it Risk-Free for 60 Days").
*   **Primary Text:** Short. Focus on the guarantee or a single powerful testimonial.
