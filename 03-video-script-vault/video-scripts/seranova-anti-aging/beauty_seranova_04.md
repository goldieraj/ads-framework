## Key Beats

1. [0:00 - 0:02] The "Triple Discount" Hook - The video opens with a clear shot of the Seranova products and a bold "FLASH SALE ENDS SOON" sticker. The voiceover immediately promises a "triple discount." Psychological Purpose: Instantly grabs attention with a high-value proposition and creates immediate curiosity about how to claim it.
2. [0:02 - 0:04] The Direct Command - The narrator says, "Tap the orange shopping cart below," accompanied by a finger pointing downwards. Psychological Purpose: Provides frictionless, explicit instructions on the exact physical action the viewer needs to take right now.
3. [0:04 - 0:06] Value Stack Part 1 (Flash Sale) - The hand picks up the microneedling device while explaining the first discount is a "flash sale." Psychological Purpose: Begins building the offer stack while simultaneously showing the physical product to build desire.
4. [0:06 - 0:09] Value Stack Part 2 (Free Shipping) - The hand picks up a pink serum vial while promising "fast and free shipping." Psychological Purpose: Removes a major buying objection (shipping costs) and adds perceived value to the offer.
5. [0:09 - 0:11] Value Stack Part 3 (Checkout Codes) - The hand shows a clear vial and then the instruction manual, mentioning "any codes you see at checkout." Psychological Purpose: Gamifies the checkout process slightly and incentivizes the user to click through just to see what extra savings they might get.
6. [0:11 - 0:15] Urgency & Final Push - The video ends with a wide shot of the kit, a finger pointing at the main box, and the warning: "You do need to hurry because the sale ends tonight, so don't miss out." Psychological Purpose: Injects strong scarcity and FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) to force an immediate purchasing decision rather than delaying.

---

## CTA / Offer

Offer: A "Triple Discount" consisting of: 1) Flash sale pricing, 2) Fast and free shipping, and 3) Additional promo codes applied at checkout. CTA: "Tap the orange shopping cart below" (to claim the discounts before the sale ends tonight).
