---

## Key Beats

1. [0:00 - 0:02] The Hook & Promise - The video starts with a shot of the branded box and the text "Triple Discount Ends tonight." The voiceover immediately promises to show the viewer how to get this deal. Psychological Purpose: Instantly grab attention with a high-value proposition ("triple discount") and create curiosity about how to claim it.
2. [0:02 - 0:04] Direct CTA & Discount #1 - The box is opened to reveal the neat packaging inside while the voiceover instructs the user to "Tap the orange shopping cart below" to unlock the first discount (Flash Sale). Psychological Purpose: Provide immediate, clear instructions on what to do next, removing friction. The unboxing visual provides a satisfying aesthetic.
3. [0:04 - 0:07] Product Reveal & Discount #2 - Hands open a smaller individual box and pull out the microneedling stamp device. The voiceover announces the second discount (Fast and free shipping). Psychological Purpose: Value stacking. While the user is visually engaged in seeing the actual tool they will receive, they are hit with another perk, increasing the perceived value of the offer.
4. [0:07 - 0:09] Component Reveal & Discount #3 - Hands pull out the small serum vial that goes with the device. The voiceover reveals the final discount (checkout codes). Psychological Purpose: Completes the "triple discount" promise while showing the consumable part of the product, reinforcing what the user is buying.
5. [0:09 - 0:14] Urgency & Final Push - The camera pans back over the full, open box of products. The voiceover states, "You need to hurry because the sale ends tonight. Don't miss out." Psychological Purpose: Inject strong scarcity and FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) to force an immediate buying decision rather than delaying it.

---

## CTA / Offer

Offer: A "Triple Discount" consisting of: 1) A Flash Sale price, 2) Fast and Free Shipping, and 3) Additional promo codes applied at checkout. CTA: "Tap the orange shopping cart below."
