# Fuel Save Pro Video Script: "The Government Conspiracy"

**Score:** 9.5/10
**Duration:** 140 seconds (~2:20)
**Hook Type:** Authority Threat + Scarcity | **Format:** 3D Animation (Pixar Style)

**Notes for AI Strategist:** Use this script template when selling a gadget or device that saves money or provides a massive shortcut, and the mechanism can be framed as a "loophole" or "secret" that big corporations want to hide.

---

## 1. The Hook (0:00 - 0:10)
**Goal:** Establish an immediate, credible threat and create massive urgency.

**Visual:** Animated politician in Oval Office slamming fists on desk. Angry expression. Points at camera. Text overlay matches voiceover.
**Voiceover:** "The government is banning this new fuel saving device next month. And this is your last shot to get the original high-powered version."

## 2. The Agitation & Villain Reveal (0:10 - 0:30)
**Goal:** Explain *why* it's being banned. Frame the corporations/government as the villain protecting their profits at the user's expense.

**Visual:** Three shady men in suits (labeled "Automotive," "Regulators," and "Startup") shaking hands in a dark room. Cut to a gas pump spinning rapidly.
**Voiceover:** "In just a few weeks, major automotive companies and regulators will force the only company that makes it to completely neuter it. That means no more driving for free. No more skipping the gas station while everyone else spends $80 every fill-up."

## 3. The Mechanism (0:30 - 1:00)
**Goal:** Explain how the product works using technical-sounding but easily understood concepts. Prove it's not a gimmick.

**Visual:** Animated car engine looking sad, then getting zapped with glowing blue/yellow "electromagnetic" energy and smiling.
**Voiceover:** "This isn't a fuel additive or a mechanic trick. It uses advanced electromagnetic technology that overrides hidden components car manufacturers secretly installed in every vehicle built after 2001. Components that deliberately waste 40% of the gas you pay for as fumes."

## 4. The Solution & Proof (1:00 - 1:30)
**Goal:** Show the product in action. Emphasize ease of use and dramatic results.

**Visual:** Hand plugging the small green device into the car's OBD2 port. A green holographic shield expands over the car. Cut to happy driver passing a gas station.
**Voiceover:** "Fuel Save Pro stops that waste completely. It's 100% legal, completely plug-and-play, and people are driving for weeks on a single tank. It was developed by a former Ford engineer with 25 years of experience."

## 5. The Offer & Urgent CTA (1:30 - 2:20)
**Goal:** Reiterate the scarcity. Force the purchase *now* before the "downgrade" happens.

**Visual:** Montage of stressed people at gas pumps, contrasted with a happy person buying the device online. Ticking clock graphic.
**Voiceover:** "Big retailers like AutoZone refuse to carry it because it would destroy their business. This current batch is the last time it will ever be sold at full power. Once the downgrade hits, the savings are gone. So why risk paying more tomorrow for a weaker version when you can grab the original right now? Click the link below before the final batch sells out."
